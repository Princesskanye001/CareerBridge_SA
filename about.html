About CareerBridge_SA

Our Mission

CareerBridge_SA is dedicated to solving South Africa's youth unemployment crisis by providing accessible virtual work experience that bridges the gap between education and employment.

Our Vision

We envision a South Africa where every young person has the opportunity to gain meaningful work experience and build a successful career, regardless of their background or connections.

What We Do

We partner with leading South African companies to create realistic work simulations that give graduates the practical skills and confidence needed to succeed in the job market.

  • Industry-relevant simulations
  • Real company projects
  • Skill verification
  • Employer connections

Our Impact

Since our launch, we've helped thousands of South African youth gain valuable work experience, with 82% of participants successfully completing our simulations and 67% securing employment within 3 months.

Simulation Completion 82%
Employment Rate (3 months) 67%
